Top 10 Grooming Products We Love

After years of grooming dogs of all breeds and temperaments, we've discovered which products truly make a difference. Here are our top 10 must-have grooming products that we use at OhRahRah and recommend for home care between professional grooming sessions.

The FURminator is a game-changer for managing shedding. It reaches deep beneath your dog's topcoat to gently remove loose undercoat hair without damaging the coat or cutting the skin.

Why we love it: Reduces shedding by up to 90% when used regularly. Available in various sizes for different coat lengths and dog sizes.

Where to buy: Pets at Home

Best for: Anxious dogs during grooming

4. Lily's Kitchen Organic Dog Shampoo

Building Your Home Grooming Kit

You don't need everything at once! Start with the basics based on your dog's coat type:

For Short-Haired Dogs:

  • Rubber grooming mitt or bristle brush
  • Nail trimmers
  • Dog shampoo for short coats
  • Microfiber towel

For Long-Haired or Double-Coated Dogs:

  • Slicker brush
  • Undercoat rake or deshedding tool
  • Metal comb
  • Detangling spray
  • Nail trimmers
  • Quality dog shampoo and conditioner

For Curly or Wire-Haired Dogs:

  • Slicker brush
  • Metal comb
  • Clippers with guard combs
  • Detangling spray
  • Specialized shampoo for curly coats
